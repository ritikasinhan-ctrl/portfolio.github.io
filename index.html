REFLECTION

My Learning Journey

An honest reflection on the challenges I faced, the skills I developed, and the lessons I carry forward.

"This module has taught me that technical skill alone is not enough — the ability to communicate, collaborate, and reflect is just as important in building a meaningful career."

Challenges I faced

One of the most consistent challenges I faced throughout this module was stepping outside my comfort zone in collaborative and communicative settings. As a naturally introverted person, contributing actively in group work and delivering presentations required deliberate effort. The Interview and CRM Role Play, in particular, pushed me to present myself confidently in a spoken, live context — something I found genuinely difficult but ultimately very valuable.

Balancing multiple module deadlines simultaneously was another challenge. Managing my time across written assignments, group projects, and exam preparation taught me the importance of planning ahead and breaking large tasks into smaller, manageable steps.

Skills I developed

This module has meaningfully strengthened my professional communication skills. Through the Business Plan, Research Paper, and Group Synopsis, I developed the ability to write formally, structure arguments logically, and cite sources correctly using Harvard referencing. I also gained confidence in collaborative working — learning to contribute my specific strengths while relying on and supporting teammates.

On a personal level, I became more aware of how I communicate and how I present myself to others. The role play activity, though challenging, showed me that professional communication is a skill that can be practised and improved — not just an innate talent.

Where I still need to improve

I recognise that spoken communication remains an area for growth. While I am comfortable expressing myself in writing, I need to continue developing my confidence in verbal communication — particularly in group settings and when speaking to people I do not know well. I also want to improve my ability to give and receive feedback constructively within a team.

Additionally, I hope to develop a stronger foundation in professional networking and industry awareness — areas that will become increasingly important as I move towards postgraduate study and a career in cybersecurity.

How I will apply this in my career

The communication and professional skills developed in ICDT1016Y form a foundation that will serve me throughout my career. In cybersecurity and blockchain — fields where complex technical concepts must be communicated clearly to clients, teams, and stakeholders — the ability to write well, present confidently, and work collaboratively is not optional. It is essential.

This module has made me a more well-rounded professional and has reinforced that the soft skills I once considered secondary are in fact among the most important I can develop.
